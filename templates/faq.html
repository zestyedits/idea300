All Questions Getting Started Billing Security & HIPAA
Who is this tool for?

This tool is designed exclusively for licensed and pre-licensed mental health professionals, including therapists, counselors, psychologists, and social workers.

Is this tool HIPAA compliant?

We are a planning tool, not an Electronic Health Record (EHR). You should never enter any Protected Health Information (PHI). Always de-identify case details. With proper use, the tool fits within HIPAA guidelines as no PHI is stored.

How does the AI work?

We use a state-of-the-art language model guided by a detailed clinical prompt developed by licensed therapists to ensure plans are structured, evidence-based, and professionally sound.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through our secure payment processor, Stripe. You can manage your subscription at any time through the secure Stripe Customer Portal.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through the billing page. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
